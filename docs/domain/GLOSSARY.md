# Glossary

Shared terminology for Smart Inquiry AI.

## Inquiry

A request submitted by a potential customer. An inquiry usually includes contact information, project details, needs, budget, timeline, and questions.

## AI Output

Derived AI-generated context associated with an inquiry. The MVP AI output contract includes summary, customer_need, urgency, missing_information, and suggested_next_action. AI output should help a team understand the customer need without replacing the customer's original submission.

## Lead

A potential customer or business opportunity. In the MVP, an inquiry may be treated as an early lead.

## Opportunity

A qualified business possibility that may become a project, deal, or customer engagement. Opportunity management belongs to the CRM phase.

## Dashboard

The internal view where the team reviews incoming inquiries, statuses, summaries, and next actions.

## Status

The current business lifecycle state of an inquiry, such as New, In Progress, Completed, or Archived.

## Attachment

A future-ready domain for files or external assets associated with an inquiry. Attachment upload is not part of the initial MVP.

## Notification

A record of an outbound notification attempt associated with an inquiry. Email is the initial delivery channel, with other channels possible later.

## CRM

Customer Relationship Management. In this project, CRM means managing customers, opportunities, notes, follow-ups, and pipeline state.

## SaaS

Software as a Service. In this project, SaaS means a hosted product that multiple businesses can sign up for and use independently.
