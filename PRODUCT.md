# Product

## Product Vision

Smart Inquiry AI helps service businesses turn incoming customer inquiries into clear, organized, and actionable work.

The product starts as a focused inquiry management tool. It captures customer requests, structures important details, and uses AI to help teams understand what the customer needs, how urgent the request is, and what should happen next.

The long-term vision is to become an AI-native workflow layer for small and medium-sized businesses, expanding from inquiry intake into CRM, follow-up, automation, and SaaS operations.

## Problem

Many small teams receive inquiries from website forms, email, chat, referrals, and manual notes. The information is often scattered, incomplete, and difficult to compare.

Common problems include:

- Teams spend too much time reading and summarizing raw inquiry messages.
- Important requests can be missed when they stay buried in email or chat.
- Follow-up actions are not always clear or consistently tracked.
- Customer context is copied manually between tools.
- Early sales opportunities are difficult to prioritize.
- Small businesses need structure without adopting a heavy CRM too early.

## Target Customer

Smart Inquiry AI is designed for small and medium-sized service businesses that handle custom requests, quotes, consultations, or project inquiries.

Primary customer segments:

- Design studios
- Web agencies
- Consulting firms
- B2B service providers
- Local professional service businesses
- Small teams that currently manage inquiries through email, spreadsheets, or chat

Primary users:

- Business owners
- Sales or account managers
- Operations staff
- Project managers
- Team members responsible for reviewing and following up with potential customers

## Value Proposition

Smart Inquiry AI helps teams respond to inquiries faster and with better context.

Core value:

- Converts unstructured inquiry messages into organized records.
- Produces AI-assisted summaries that reduce manual review time.
- Highlights customer needs, urgency, and suggested next actions.
- Keeps inquiry status visible in one place.
- Creates a foundation for future CRM and workflow automation.

For the MVP, the product should feel lighter than a CRM but more reliable than email and spreadsheets.

## MVP Scope

The MVP focuses on one complete inquiry workflow from submission to internal review.

Included in MVP:

- Public inquiry submission form
- Basic customer contact information
- Inquiry message and request details
- AI output using the initial MVP contract
- Email notification when a new inquiry is received
- Internal inquiry dashboard
- Inquiry detail view
- Basic status tracking

Initial AI output contract:

- summary
- customer_need
- urgency
- missing_information
- suggested_next_action

AI generation failure must never prevent an Inquiry from being created.

Initial inquiry statuses:

- New
- In Progress
- Completed
- Archived

Not included in MVP:

- Full CRM pipeline
- Attachment upload
- Multi-tenant SaaS accounts
- Billing
- Authentication for the first demo implementation
- Advanced permissions
- Custom workflow builder
- Calendar integration
- Email inbox integration
- Analytics dashboard

MVP success means a team can receive an inquiry, understand it quickly, and know what follow-up action to take without relying on scattered manual notes.
